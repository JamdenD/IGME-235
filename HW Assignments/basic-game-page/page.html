The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time follows a young boy named Link who is sent on a quest to help Princess Zelda after being told about a grave danger threatening his land of Hyrule. Link must go on an adventure across Hyrule and time to stop Ganondorf and save the Kingdom!

Publishing Info

  • Published by: Nintendo Co.
  • Developed by: Nintendo EAD
  • Released: 1998 on Nintendo 64

Additional Info

  • Genre: Action
  • Perspective: 1st person, Behind view
  • Gameplay: 3D exploration, puzzle solving
  • ESRB Rating: Everyone

Quote

"The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time should be recommended playing for ever aspiring videogame designer and programmer out there. If you're making games and you haven't played this game, then you're like a director who has never seen Citizen Kane or a musician who has never heard of Mozart." -IGN
